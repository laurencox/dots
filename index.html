Specimens

Dots is an ornamental icon set that I designed for my senior graphic design thesis project at Montserrat College Of Art. These icons were created by overlapping letters from the notepad typewriter drawings I made. It consists of every lowercase letter of the alphabet. I wanted people to be able to download and use them for their projects and also be able to change the color. They can be used for many different things such as borders in a document, bullet points, dividers between sections in a chapter of a book, and even as a textural brush in PhotoShop or Illustrator.
